People

Manolinh thepkhamvong

Managing Director

Mr. Manolinh Thepkhamvong, He finished Bachelor of Law major in judicial law in 2001 from Faculty of Law and Political Science, National of University of Laos and Business Administration in 2005 from ComCenter College in Laos. Lao Bar Association member in 2006, from 2007 to 2009 Legal Advocacy Officer on Land project for Village Focus International (VFI). 2009-2011 a Freelancer-Legal Consultant and involved various projects in Laos on legal aid and promote people’s access to justice and rule of law. 2011-2014, he studied Master of Science major in Development Management and Governance at the University of the Philippines Los Banos. Late 2014, he established The Law and Development Partnership(LDP) and Legal Aid Lawyer Group(LLG), Mr. Thepkhamvong has been working as Pro bono lawyer at in-House Consultation Clinic, Faculty of Law and Political Science (FLP) and supports Association for Development of Women and Legal Education(ADWLE) to establish first NPA legal clinic. In 2014, he was selected to serve as Lao PDR’s representative for the Baseline Study on Legal Aid in Southeast Asia, focus on Legal Aid in Lao PDR, and currently he is working as a consultant on Legal Training Development Expert for UNODC project( the LaoX26) to support OSPP on the development and delivery of the prosecutor training enhancement program on Human Trafficking.


Tip Nuanseevong

Legel Adviser and Pro Bono Program

Thip Nouansyvong is BABSEA CLE Laos National CLE Coordinator. She is an attorney admitted to the Laos Bar Association. Ms. Nouansyvong has been working with BABSEA CLE since 2009. In 2012, she has been led Local Organizing Committee for 1st Southeast Asia Pro Bono Conference in Laos, and being International Organizing Committee Members for Annual Asia Pro Bono Conference in Vietnam (2013), Singapore (2014) and Myanmar (2015). In her current role, she assists in the strengthening of the CLE Programme at the National University of Laos, Faculty of Law and Political Science, which includes the implementation of an In-House Consultation Clinic, Community Teaching programmes and expansion of the Community Legal Education Programme. She has also been a leader in designing and implementing a nationwide plan for CLE expansion throughout Laos. .
